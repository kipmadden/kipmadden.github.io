About


I founded CoSapient to help businesses take advantage of the incredible power of Digital Marketing
to gain and nurture new customers. Digital Marketing has eclipsed Traditional Marketing because of its
higher ROI, reduced cost, easy measurement and ability to specifically target your ideal buyer persona.

I help any size business compete with larger competitors.


My three-pronged approach consists of:

  1. Consulting with you to gain a deep understanding of your business and your competitors.
  2. Next, we use the KWPs to design a clean and responsive user-friendly website with a sales funnel that culminates with Calls-To-Action to engage potential customers.
  3. Finally, we increase your website traffic by growing your brand awareness and domain authority through our On-site and Off-site SEO efforts combined with well managed PPC campaigns.
